- Bowling game: Struggles with the physics of the bowling ball, the pins, and the surrounding bowling alley (ball clips through walls). Claude Opus 4.6's [Try](https://u9g.github.io/bowlroyale/) [Code](https://github.com/u9g/bowlroyale)

- Styling a website in the style of another website given as URL: Struggles with the understanding of the design. At this point, Claude is pretty good at reading and writing code, but the visual understanding of an image leaves much to be desired, which I believe to be the current bottleneck. A good benchmark for this would be to take [this repository](https://github.com/u9g/jason-portfolio) and say 'change the styling to be in the style of red.anthropic.com (or windows explorer)'. Both of which will currently fail, only being able to produce minimal nods to the design inspiration with no real designer feel of how to be truly inspired.
